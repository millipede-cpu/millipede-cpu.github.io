About Me

Lauren Caldwell is an English Freelance Illustrator, living in Northen Italy. Over years of developing her artistic skills, she now favours the use of coloured pencils, fineliners and acrylic paints as her chosen mediums to work with. Lauren received a BA(Hons) Illustration Degree in 2013. Since graduating, Lauren has been working as a Freelance Illustrator and has travelled half the world, gathering inspiration from everything around her. During the months of the global lockdown, Lauren started a self-initiated project to celebrate essential workers and to share the actions of the British political leaders. Lauren's recent artwork gives you a powerful gaze into the worst pandemic in our lifetime.

For commission enquiries, please email Lauren by clicking the Contact Me or Facebook links at the top of the page.

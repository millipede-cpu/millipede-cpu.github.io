About the Artist

Lauren Caldwell is an English Freelance Illustrator, living in Northen Italy. She has developed her artistic skills to perfection over the years, favouring coloured pencils and fineliners as her chosen medium to work with. She has been drawing, painting and expressing her creative side her whole life and she received a BA(Hons) Illustration Degree in 2013. Since graduating, Lauren works as a Freelance Illustrator and has travelled half the world, gathering inspiration from everything around her. During the months of the global lockdown, Lauren started a self-initiated project to celebrate essential workers and to share the actions of the political leaders of the world. Lauren's recent artwork gives you a powerful gaze into the worst pandemic in our lifetime.

For commission enquires, please email Lauren by clicking the Contact Me or Facebook links at the top of the page.
